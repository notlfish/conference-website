OCaml Developers' Global Conference 2022

The OCaml Users and Developers Workshop brings together industrial users of OCaml with academics and hackers who are working on extending the language, type system, and tools. Previous editions have been co-located with ICFP 2019 in Berlin, and was virtual for ICFP 2020, following the OCaml Meetings in Paris in 2010 and 2011.

Please contact per Email for any further questions about OCaml conference 2022!

occonferece@camlhump

OCaml Conference Main Topic

The main topic of this conference is the long-awaited Multicore Ocaml, by unanimous choice in the developers' poll. Multicore Ocaml was released last June and it's going to be discussed at length by the designers at INRIA and OCamlLabs, alongside algebraic effects.

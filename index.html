Objective Caml Developers' Global Conference 2022

A friendly gathering of OCaml developers all over the world, to learn, discuss and share the works of each other and of the language contributors. Held in October, in Prague.

2022.10.12(Wed) ~ 15(Sat)

@ Kafka Conference Center, Kvitova Meeting Center and more

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers of various countries about all kinds of OCaml-related topics

Applications Exhibition

Appreciate various creations using OCaml, by independent developers and different teams

Forum

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic

Workshop

Try creating your own work using OCaml rather than just watching

OCaml Ignite

Get opportunities to network with OCaml developers around the world, also after the summit

Featured Speakers

Oleg Kiselyov

http://okmij.org/ftp/

Known for his work with type systems, functional languanges and the puzzling and awesome code in his wegpage.

Anil Madhavapeddy

University of Cambridge

University Lecturer at the University of Cambridge. He's worked in a variety of senior architecture, engineering, research, and more.

Mehdi Bouaziz

Nomadic Labs Paris

PhD student of the École normale supérieure in Paris. He's working on static analysis of security properties by abstract interpretation.

Youyou Cong

Tokyo Institute of Technology

Professor at Tokyo Institute of Technology. Her research concentrates on the theory of programming languages, with a particular interest in continuations and dependent types.

Kihong Heo

KAIST

Assistant Professor in Programming Systems Laboratory of KAIST. Looking for students in program analysis, program synthesis/transformation, and machine learning

Jonathan Protzenko

Microsoft Research

Principal Researcher in the RiSE group at Microsoft Research Redmond. His research now focuses on formally verifying critical software.
